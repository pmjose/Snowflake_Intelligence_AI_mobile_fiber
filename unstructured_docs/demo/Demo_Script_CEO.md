# CEO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - Gamma Communications PLC

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CEO/Board-level audiences at Gamma Communications, focusing on strategic insights, market position, and business performance based on the latest H1 2025 results and FTSE 250 positioning.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll show you how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform enables instant access to strategic insights across all of Gamma's business operations - combining operational data with strategy documents in natural language. This is particularly timely as Gamma has just achieved FTSE 250 status following our Main Market move in May 2025."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Business Health Overview
> "Give me an executive summary of Gamma's H1 2025 performance - revenue, cloud seats, adjusted EBITDA, and our key growth metrics."

**Expected Insights:**
- £316.6M H1 revenue, up 12% YoY
- 1.8 million total Cloud seats, +50% YoY
- £70.9M Adjusted EBITDA, +14% growth
- 54% gross margin, up 2pp
- 90% recurring revenue
- Germany now 20% of Group gross profit

**Talking Point:** "One question gives you the complete health of the business including our transformational German expansion."

---

### Question 2: Germany Performance
> "How is our German business performing following the Starface and Placetel acquisitions?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Germany gross profit: £34.4M (H1 2025) vs £9.8M (H1 2024) - up 251%
- 565,000 cloud seats in Germany (vs 38,000 a year ago)
- 70.1% gross margin (highest in group)
- Double-digit proforma revenue growth in both Starface and Placetel
- Germany now represents c.20% of Group gross profit
- 29,000 organic seat additions on proforma basis

**Talking Point:** "Our strategic bet on Germany is delivering exceptional results - the market is underpenetrated for cloud and we're now one of the leading providers."

---

### Question 3: Market Position
> "How is Gamma positioned against 8x8, RingCentral, and Mitel? What opportunities does competitor weakness create?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Gamma UK market share now ~12%, growing (vs 10% in 2024)
- 8x8: Financial pressures, cost restructuring - partner recruitment opportunity
- RingCentral: Channel conflict - SMB/Mid-Market positioning advantage
- Mitel: Cloud transition struggles - migration target
- Gamma now in FTSE 250 - credibility and scale advantage
- Partner NPS significantly higher than competitors (+52 vs ~+28 industry avg)

**Talking Point:** "Real-time competitive intelligence shows we're gaining share as competitors struggle."

---

### Question 4: Microsoft Teams & Strategic Products
> "What is our progress on Microsoft Teams Phone, Cisco Collaboration, and the PSTN switch-off opportunity?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Teams Phone: 523K users in UK (+12% in H1), 17K in Europe (+21%)
- Added 56K UK Teams users in H1 (vs 28K in H1 2024) - doubled the rate
- Cisco Collaboration Suite: 28K users, up 75% in half, full launch October 2025
- PhoneLine+: 45K seats, +32% in half (PSTN replacement solution)
- PSTN opportunity: c.40% UK market still to migrate to cloud
- Operator Connect International launched - 14 European countries

**Talking Point:** "Our Microsoft and Cisco partnerships position us at the centre of enterprise cloud communications."

---

### Question 5: Strategic Outlook
> "What are the key metrics for the board and what is our FY 2025 outlook?"

**Expected Insights:**
- FY 2025 Adjusted EBITDA: £139-143M (consensus)
- FY 2025 Adjusted EPS: 89.9p-93.9p (consensus), slightly ahead expected
- UK restructuring: £6-8M annual cost savings from H2
- Near-term headwinds: PSTN migration (~£3M), ethernet pricing (~£3M) in FY26
- Germany expected to continue strong growth
- AI features launching Q4 2025 (AI Receptionist)
- Share buyback completed: £45.1M in H1

**Talking Point:** "Board-ready insights in seconds - we're confident in meeting expectations despite UK macro pressures."

---

## Closing (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"In 10 minutes, you've seen how this platform:
- Provides instant strategic insights from our H1 2025 results
- Tracks our German transformation in real-time
- Enables competitive analysis across UK and European markets
- Supports FTSE 250 board-level decision making

As Andrew Belshaw noted in the H1 results: 'Our robust business model, based on high recurring revenue from solutions critical to the businesses that use them, leaves us well placed to maximise the opportunities ahead.'

This platform brings that insight to your fingertips."
