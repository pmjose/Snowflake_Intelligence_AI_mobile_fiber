# CFO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - Gamma Communications PLC

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CFO and Finance leadership at Gamma Communications, focusing on H1 2025 financial performance, segment profitability, partner revenue analysis, and German acquisition integration metrics.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll demonstrate how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform provides instant access to financial insights - from our H1 2025 results through to segment profitability, German acquisition performance, and cash generation - all through natural language queries. This is particularly relevant as we've transitioned to FTSE 250 reporting standards."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: H1 2025 Financial Performance
> "What was our H1 2025 financial performance? Show me revenue, gross profit by business unit, and adjusted EBITDA."

**Expected Insights:**
- Total Revenue: £316.6M (+12% YoY)
- Gross Profit: £172.0M (+18% YoY)
- Gross Margin: 54% (+2pp YoY)
- **By Business Unit:**
  - Gamma Business: £97.4M GP (57% of total)
  - Gamma Enterprise: £30.9M GP (18% of total)
  - Germany: £34.4M GP (20% of total) - up 251%!
  - Other Europe: £9.3M GP (5% of total)
- Adjusted EBITDA: £70.9M (22.4% margin)
- Adjusted EPS: 47.9p (+13% YoY)

**Talking Point:** "Complete P&L visibility by segment in one question - Germany transformation clearly visible."

---

### Question 2: Germany Acquisition Performance
> "How are the Starface and Placetel acquisitions performing? What are the integration financials?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Combined Germany gross profit: £34.4M (H1 2025) vs £9.8M (H1 2024)
- Gross margin: 70.1% (highest in group) - no licensing costs
- Double-digit proforma revenue growth in both acquisitions
- Germany operating costs: £24.1M (up £17.1M due to acquisitions)
- Organic constant currency GP growth: +4%
- 565,000 cloud seats (proforma growth 5% in H1)
- Amortisation of M&A intangibles: £9.6M (up 52% due to acquisitions)

**Talking Point:** "Acquisition performance tracked in real-time - Starface and Placetel exceeding expectations."

---

### Question 3: Cash Flow & Balance Sheet
> "What is our cash position, cash conversion, and net debt following the acquisitions and share buyback?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Adjusted cash generated: £63.7M (+2% YoY)
- Adjusted cash conversion: 90% (vs 100% in H1 2024)
- Cash deployed:
  - Share buyback completed: £45.1M
  - Starface acquisition: c.£175M
  - 2024 buyback: £27.3M
- Net debt position: (£21.6M) vs £142.9M net cash (H1 2024)
- Net Debt/EBITDA: 0.15x - strong deleveraging capacity
- Dividend: 7.4p interim (+14% YoY)

**Talking Point:** "Despite major acquisition, net debt minimal - demonstrates cash generation strength."

---

### Question 4: Segment & Partner Economics
> "Show me revenue contribution by partner tier and gross profit margins by business unit."

**Expected Insights:**
- **Partner Revenue (UK Business):**
  - Service Provider: £43.5M revenue, £21.3M GP (22% of Business)
  - Channel: £142.5M revenue (78% of Business)
  - 1,500+ active partners
- **Gross Margins by Unit:**
  - Germany: 70.1% (highest - owned IP, no mobile)
  - Other Europe: 62%
  - Gamma Business: 52.4%
  - Gamma Enterprise: 46.5%
- **Margin Headwinds:**
  - Copper-to-fibre migration: £1.5M GP impact
  - Ethernet price war: £1.0M GP impact
  - Estimated FY26 GP headwind: £6M combined

**Talking Point:** "Germany's 70% margin transforms group profitability mix."

---

### Question 5: FY 2025 Outlook & Cost Actions
> "What are our FY 2025 guidance metrics and what cost actions are we taking?"

**Expected Insights:**
- **Consensus Expectations:**
  - Adjusted EBITDA: £139.4M - £143.1M (Board: "in line")
  - Adjusted EPS: 89.9p - 93.9p (Board: "slightly ahead")
- **UK Restructuring (H2 2025):**
  - Headcount reduction: Up to 5% of Group
  - Annual cost savings: £6-8M from FY26
  - Exceptional restructuring cost: c.£3M (H2 2025)
- **Headwinds Quantified:**
  - PSTN migration: c.£3M GP pressure to FY26
  - Ethernet pricing: c.£3M GP pressure into FY26
- **Operating Expense Movements:**
  - Central costs down £2.3M (contingent consideration release)
  - R&D costs down £3.1M (normalised capitalisation)

**Talking Point:** "Full transparency on guidance, headwinds, and mitigating actions - investor-ready."
