# CMO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - Gamma Communications

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CMO and Marketing leadership at Gamma Communications, focusing on campaign performance, partner marketing, competitive intelligence, and customer satisfaction metrics.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll demonstrate how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform enables marketing leaders to instantly access campaign performance, competitive intelligence, and customer insights - all through natural language queries."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Campaign ROI Analysis
> "Which marketing campaigns delivered the best ROI in Q4 2024? Show me spend, leads, and pipeline."

**Expected Insights:**
- PSTN Switch-Off: £850K spend, 2,100 MQLs, 16.5x ROI
- Teams Phone Push: £620K spend, 1,450 MQLs, 15.3x ROI
- Partner Recruitment: £380K spend, 185 partners, 21.1x ROI
- Total Q4: £4.2M spend, 6,850 MQLs, 11.4x ROI

**Talking Point:** "Campaign optimisation decisions in real-time."

---

### Question 2: Partner Marketing Performance
> "How effective is our MDF programme? Which partners are using it best?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Total MDF deployed: £450K
- MQLs generated: 1,200
- Pipeline created: £7.5M
- Platinum partners: best utilisation (100%)
- Gold partners: 75% utilisation
- ROI: 16.7x on MDF investment

**Talking Point:** "Partner enablement ROI tracked precisely."

---

### Question 3: Customer Satisfaction Trends
> "What's our NPS trend and how do we compare to competitors like 8x8 and RingCentral?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Customer NPS: +45 (up 5 points YoY)
- Partner NPS: +52 (up 4 points YoY)
- vs 8x8: +28 (we're +17 ahead)
- vs RingCentral: +32 (we're +13 ahead)
- Key driver: Partner relationship quality

**Talking Point:** "Customer voice combined with competitive benchmarking."

---

### Question 4: Competitive Intelligence
> "What are the key competitive threats and opportunities from 8x8's strategic review?"

**Expected Insights:**
- 8x8 partner programme under pressure
- Partner margins reduced
- UK headcount cuts
- Opportunity: target top 50 8x8 partners
- Response: partner recruitment campaign active

**Talking Point:** "Competitive intelligence from documents and market data combined."

---

### Question 5: Channel Effectiveness
> "Which marketing channels deliver the best cost per lead and conversion rates?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Email: £144 CPL, 4.5% conversion (best efficiency)
- LinkedIn: £456 CPL, 2.8% conversion (quality leads)
- Webinars: £405 CPL, 12.5% conversion (best quality)
- Events: £1,101 CPL, 8% conversion (relationship building)

**Talking Point:** "Channel mix optimisation driven by data."

---

## Closing (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**

- Tracks partner marketing effectiveness
- Monitors customer satisfaction vs competitors
- Provides competitive intelligence on demand

This is how modern marketing operates - data-driven, real-time, and connected."
