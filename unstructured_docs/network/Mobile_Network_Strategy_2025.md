# Mobile Network Strategy 2025
**Snowmobile Telco - 4G/5G Network Roadmap**

## Executive Summary

Snowmobile Telco is investing £85 million in mobile network infrastructure over 2025-2027 to deliver best-in-class 4G and 5G connectivity for UK businesses. As an MVNO with strategic partnerships with major UK carriers, we are focused on delivering enterprise-grade mobile services with superior coverage, reliability, and business-focused features.
